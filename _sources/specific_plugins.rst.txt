GView Specific Plugins (Type Plugins)
=====================================

Type plugins parse specific file formats and provide smart viewers and panels.
The following pages describe selected plugins and their references:

Other supported types include PE, ELF, BMP, ICO, LNK, JOB, MAM, ISO, PCAP, CSV, PDF, ZIP, and more.
